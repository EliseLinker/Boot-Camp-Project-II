Results

Although kMeans didn't find a one-to-one relationship between each of the 12 wine types and clusters, we were still able to come up with clusters that represented some wines like Bordeaux, Chardonay, Cabernet Sauvignon, Pinot Noir, and Riesling. This allows us to see how people are generally describing these kinds of wines, which is pretty cool!
